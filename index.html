Turn 0 - Defender's Turn

Game Overview

  • This is a turn-based point defense system where defenders (blue) must protect against incoming attackers (red)
  • The game alternates between defender and attacker turns, shown at the bottom of the screen
  • Your goal is to protect the defenders by anticipating and intercepting attacker movements

Defender's Turn

  • During your turn, you can target multiple tiles that you think attackers will move to
  • Click on any tile to target it - it will be marked with a red X
  • Targeted tiles will be fired upon in the next turn
  • You cannot target tiles containing defenders or current attacker positions
  • Use the numbered paths to predict where attackers will move next
  • Click "Next Turn" when you're done targeting

Attacker's Turn

  • Attackers follow predetermined paths shown by colored dashed lines
  • Each attacker moves at a speed of 1, 2, or 3 tiles per turn
  • Numbers along the path show the sequence of movement
  • If an attacker moves onto a targeted tile, it is destroyed
  • If an attacker reaches a defender's position, that defender is eliminated
  • Attackers cannot change their path or speed once chosen

Game Rules & Strategy

  • New attackers appear every 20 turns from the top of the grid
  • Attackers must spawn at least 20 tiles away from any defender
  • Study the numbered paths carefully to predict attacker movements
  • Try to target tiles where multiple attackers might intersect
  • The game continues until all defenders are eliminated
  • Use "New Game" to start a fresh game at any time

Visual Guide

  • Blue Rectangles: Defenders (stationary)
  • Red Diamonds: Attackers (moving)
  • Colored Dashed Lines: Attacker paths
  • White Numbers: Movement sequence
  • Red X: Targeted tiles
  • Green Highlight: Hover indicator for targeting
